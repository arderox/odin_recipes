Description

If you can't go to NY, make NY come to you.

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups or 639g of bread flour.
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar.
  • 3 teaspoons sea salt.
  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast.
  • 1 1/2 cups or 340ml of lukewarm water.
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil.
  • Pizza sauce.
  • Mozarella.

The Day Before

  1. In a bowl of an electric mixer add the flour, sugar salt and yeast.
  2. In a separate jug add the warm water and oil.
  3. Turn the machine on to medium speed andd the wet ingredients into the dry. Just add enough liquid for the dough to form a ball.
  4. When the dough is ready, divide the dough into three balls and place each in a container with a sealable lid. Place in refrigerator and allow to rise at least one day and up to 4 days. The dough can also be frozen at this stage.

The Next Day

  1. At least two hours before baking, remove dough from the refrigerator and allow it sit at room temperature to take the chill of it.
  2. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F and place in a pizza stoner or tray if using. Turn a dough ball out into a lightly floured surface. Gently stretch the dough by draping over your knuckles into a 12-14 inch circle about 1/4-inch thick. Slide the pizza base onto a flat baking tray lined with parchment.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the surface of the crust, almost to the edge. Sprinkle cheese over sauce followed by any of your favorite pizza toppings.
  4. Slide pizza onto baking stone and bake for roughly 12-15 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown. Transfer to cutting board, slice and serve immediately.
